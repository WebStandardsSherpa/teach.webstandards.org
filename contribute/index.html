Contribute a Learning Module

A learning module focuses on a concept, topic or skill that can be typically taught in the duration of one or two weeks in a standard semester-based course. Learning modules relate directly to competences that are part of a course. Use our template as a guide to help you get started on the learning module you want to share. Post your completed module on your server, then send us the link with the form below.
